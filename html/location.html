By Air

Nearest Airport

Lucknow - Chaudhary Charan Singh Airport

Distance: 170 kilometers from Shravasti

Connectivity

Well-connected to major Indian cities:

  • New Delhi (1 hour)
  • Mumbai (2 hours)
  • Bangalore (2.5 hours)
  • Chennai (3 hours)
  • Agra, Kolkata, and other major cities

From Lucknow Airport to Shravasti

  • Taxi/Cab: Direct hire (‚Çπ3500-4500, 4-5 hours)
  • Bus: Go to Alambagh bus stand, then take bus to Balrampur/Gonda
  • Train: Take train to Gonda/Balrampur from Lucknow Junction
Pro Tip: Book your onward journey (taxi/bus) in advance, especially during peak pilgrimage season (October-March).

By Train

Nearest Railway Stations

1. Balrampur Railway Station (Recommended)

This is the closest station with decent connectivity.

2. Gonda Railway Station (Alternative)

Major Train Connections to Balrampur

  • From Delhi: Balrampur Express, Gonda Express
  • From Lucknow: Multiple passenger trains daily
  • From Mumbai: Via Lucknow/Gonda
  • From Kolkata: Via Gorakhpur/Gonda

From Railway Station to Shravasti

  • From Balrampur: Local taxi/auto (‚Çπ300-500, 30 mins)
  • From Gonda: Taxi (‚Çπ800-1200, 1 hour) or bus to Balrampur
Pro Tip: Book tickets well in advance. Check train availability on IRCTC or use apps like RailYatri for real-time updates.

By Road

Road Connectivity

Shravasti is well-connected by roadways. National Highway 730A passes through the region.

State Transport Buses

UP State Road Transport Corporation (UPSRTC) operates regular bus services:

  • From Lucknow: Direct buses available (‚Çπ200-350, 5-6 hours)
  • From Gonda: Frequent buses (‚Çπ80-150, 1.5 hours)
  • From Bareilly, Kanpur, Allahabad: Via Lucknow

Private Buses & Taxis

  • Private Operators: More comfortable options from Lucknow
  • Shared Taxis: Available from Gonda/Balrampur
  • Cab Services: Ola/Uber outstation from Lucknow

Self-Drive

If driving from Lucknow:

  • Route: Lucknow ‚Üí Sitapur ‚Üí Lakhimpur ‚Üí Gonda ‚Üí Balrampur ‚Üí Shravasti
  • Distance: ~170 km
  • Time: 4-5 hours
  • Road Condition: Mostly good, some patches under construction
Pro Tip: Start early morning to avoid traffic and reach before afternoon. Roads are generally safe but drive cautiously in rural stretches.

Travel Tips

Essential Information for Visitors

üéí What to Carry

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen, sunglasses)
  • Water bottle
  • Camera and extra batteries
  • Light cotton clothes (white/light colored preferred)
  • Shawl/scarf for temple visits
  • Basic medicines and first-aid
  • Mobile charger and power bank

üôè Cultural Etiquette

  • Remove shoes before entering temples
  • Dress modestly, cover shoulders and knees
  • Maintain silence in meditation areas
  • Don't touch Buddhist statues
  • Photography rules: Ask before clicking
  • Respect monks and pilgrims
  • No smoking or alcohol near temples
  • Clockwise circumambulation of stupas

üí° Helpful Tips

  • Start temple visits early morning (6-8 AM)
  • Hire local guide for better insights (‚Çπ500-1000/day)
  • Carry sufficient cash (limited ATMs)
  • Learn basic Hindi phrases
  • Stay hydrated, especially in summer
  • Respect photography restrictions
  • Try local vegetarian cuisine
  • Keep emergency contacts handy
